About Me

Hello! I'm Torsten Mahr, a Technology Strategist at Microsoft obsessed with AI transformation in enterprise banking. I live and breathe AI adoption day in, day out—working with one of Germany's largest banks to push the boundaries of what's possible.

I'm that person who gets genuinely excited talking about AI assistants handling thousands of customer inquiries, intelligent automation transforming back-office operations, and how generative AI can revolutionize everything from risk assessment to customer experience. My passion is taking bleeding-edge AI capabilities and making them work in the real world of regulated banking—where compliance, security, and reliability aren't optional. It will change the way we think about banking, and I'm here to make sure it happens right. AI isn't just a buzzword for me; it's the future of how we interact with financial services, and I'm committed to leading that charge.

Before Microsoft, I led the Cloud Center of Excellence at Volkswagen Financial Services, building cloud strategy from the ground up and launching the Enabler Tribe, a platform-driven organization delivering services across the VW Financial Group. The common thread? Turning ambition into architecture and creating trust that makes transformation real.
